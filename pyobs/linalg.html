Linear Algebra routines¶

Matrix and vector products¶

einsum(subscripts, *operands)

Evaluates the Einstein summation convention on the input observables or arrays.

Matrix eigenvalues¶

eig(x)

Computes the eigenvalues and eigenvectors of a square matrix observable.

eigLR(x)

Computes the eigenvalues and the left and right eigenvectors of a square matrix observable.

Matrix inverse and powers¶

inv(x)

Compute the inverse of a square matrix

matrix_power(x, a)

Raises a square symmetric matrix to any non-integer power a.
